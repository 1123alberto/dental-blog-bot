📢 Vaping and the Oral Microbiome: The Hidden Dental Risks of E-Cigarettes

The transition from traditional smoking to electronic cigarettes is often viewed as a step toward harm reduction. However, from a microbiological perspective, the mouth does not recognize a "safer" alternative. A comprehensive systematic review published in theJournal of Clinical Periodontologyhighlights a troubling development: e-cigarette aerosol significantly alters theoral microbiota, the delicate ecosystem of beneficial and harmful bacteria living in our mouths.
